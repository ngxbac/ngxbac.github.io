My name is Bac Nguyen. I am a Senior Research Engineer of Applied team at [VinAI Research Vietnam](https://www.vinai.io). Prior to VinAI, I pursued Master degree at Chonnam Nation University, South Korea under supervision of Prof. [Gueesang Lee](http://eng.jnu.ac.kr/user/indexSub.action?codyMenuSeq=9176&siteId=eng_eng&menuUIType=sub&dum=dum&command=view&profSeq=439). Before falling in love with Artifical Intelligent (AI), I was an Embedded Software Engineer working in Japan for 2 years.

My research topics now are focusing on 3D Object Detection, Facial Recognition, Medical Image Processing and Emotion Recognition.
