Xuan-Bac Nguyen

I am currently a PhD student in the Computer Science and Computer Engineering Department at the University of Arkansas, under the supervision of Dr. Khoa Luu. I previously earned my M.S. in Computer Science from Chonnam National University, advised by Prof. Gueesang Lee, and my B.Sc. in Electronics and Telecommunications from the University of Engineering and Technology, VNU. My research interests include computer vision, vision-brain understanding, and quantum machine learning.
