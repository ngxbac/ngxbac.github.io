Xuan-Bac Nguyen

VinAI Research Vietnam. 14 Thuy Khue, Hanoi, Vietnam

My name is Bac Nguyen. I am a Senior Research Engineer of Applied team at VinAI Research Vietnam. Prior to VinAI, I pursued Master degree at Chonnam Nation University, South Korea under supervision of Prof. Gueesang Lee. Before falling in love with Artifical Intelligent (AI), I was an Embedded Software Engineer working in Japan for 2 years.

My research topics now are focusing on 3D Object Detection, Facial Recognition, Medical Image Processing and Emotion Recognition.
